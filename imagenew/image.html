IronMan-Tony Stark--- A fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. He is known for his technologically advanced suit of armor, which grants him superhuman strength, flight, and a variety of weapons.
Hulk-Bruce Banner--- Hulk is the alter ego of Dr. Bruce Banner, a brilliant but emotionally volatile scientist.He is often depicted as a conflicted character, struggling with his inner monster.The Hulk is a powerful member of the Avengers and a staple of the Marvel Universe.
Captain America-Steve Rogers--- His super strength, agility, and fighting skills, all enhanced by the Super-Soldier Serum. He is a symbol of patriotism and stands for justice and freedom. He was a key member of the Avengers and led the team in many battles.
Thor-Odinson--- The god of thunder, wielding the powerful hammer Mjolnir. He is known for his strength and role as a protector of both Asgard and Midgard. Thor's hammer, Mjolnir, grants him control over storms and is only wieldable by those deemed worthy.
